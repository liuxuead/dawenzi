Game Manual

1. Controls

  1. Cannon Rotation - Drag: Drag near the cannon to rotate and aim --- PC: Mouse drag
  2. Fire Cannon - Double-tap/Click: Double-tap near the cannon to fire --- PC: Single click
  3. Laser Attack - Long Press: Long press on screen for 1 second --- PC: Right-click long press for 1 second
  4. Missile Launch - Click: Click on or near a mosquito, missile will auto-track --- PC: Right-click

2. Mosquitoes

  1. Speed Mosquito: Faster than other mosquitoes, recommend using missiles
  2. Clone Mosquito (Largest): Can clone any other mosquito randomly, eliminate it quickly
  3. Healer Mosquito: Can heal thick-blood mosquitoes
  4. Thick-blood Mosquito (with health bar): Cannot be killed with one hit, be careful
  5. Invisible Mosquito: Has stealth ability
